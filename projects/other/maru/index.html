About Me

Born and raised in New York, Marusol received her Bachelor of Fine Arts at New York University. She decided the best way to use her very expensive degree was to dive headfirst into game design. Aside from video games, Marusol is an avid fan of psychological thriller anime and Kpop superstars, BTS. She is currently working on earning her coding degree, creating small indie games, and paying off her exorbitant student loans.
